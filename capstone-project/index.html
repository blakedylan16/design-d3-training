Exploring the Relationship Between Education and Poverty Levels in New York State

Discoveries

In 2020, the counties that have higher education attainment rates (ages 25 and older) tend to also have higher poverty rates (ages 0-17).
Education attainment rates (ages 25 and older) and poverty rates (ages 0-17) had a notable net decrease from 2009 to 2020.
In 2020, cities in NY tend to show higher percentages of both education attainment rates (ages 25 and older) and poverty rates (ages 0-17) compared to those of rural areas and towns.
